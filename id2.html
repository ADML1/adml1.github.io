This was an epic battle between two titans of the ADML in 2013. The reigning 2012 ADML Champion Gas House Gorillas (100-58) versus the Carolina Blue (103-55). Will the dominant pitching on both teams play out in the series?

Game One at Carolina
GG Wins 5-2 (GG leads series 1-0)
W - Cain (1-0)
L - Verlander (0-1)
SV - Kimbrel (1)
HR - GG: Ruggiano (1), Moss (1), CB: None

Game Two at Carolina
GG Wins 3-1 (GG leads series 2-0)
W - Henderson (1-0)
L - Hernandez (0-1)
SV - Kimbrel (2)
HR - GG: Phillips (1), CB: None

Game Three at Gas House
GG Wins 3-2 in 10 Innings (GG leads series 3-0)
W - Robertson (1-0)
L - Parnell (0-1)
HR - GG: None, CB: None

Game Four at Gas House
CB Wins 6-1 (GG leads series 3-1)
W - Beachy (1-0)
L - Johnson (0-1)
HR - GG: None, CB: Youkilis 2 (2)

Game Five at Gas House
GG Wins 3-2 (GG wins series 4-1)
W - Cain (2-0)
L - Verlander (0-2)
SV - Kimbrel (3)
HR - GG: Hunter (1), CB: None

The Gas House Gorillas win their second consecutive ADML1 championship.  RHP Matt Cain is selected as World Series MVP with a 2-0 record, 2.77 ERA, 13 IP and 12 K.

Congrats to Ed Corbell and the Carolina franchise on a great season, it is hard to get this far into the playoffs.
